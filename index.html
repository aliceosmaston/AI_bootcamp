About Me

Bio

I'm a final‑year PhD candidate at University College London researching the genetic basis of tuberculosis pathology and transmission in Peru. With experience across academia, biotech start‑ups, and global health projects, I combine wet‑lab expertise with data‑driven insight to improve human health. Fluent in Spanish after 2.5 years of immersive fieldwork in Peru.

  • Academia: PhD in Genetic Epidemiology (ongoing), MSc Applied Infectious Disease Epidemiology (Distinction), BSc Cellular & Molecular Medicine (First‑Class Hons).
  • Industry experience: Illumina Accelerator, Mitra Bio, Aarogya AI, Neurolytic Healthcare.
  • Technical toolkit: Illumina & Nanopore sequencing, Hamilton automation, R, Stata, SPSS, AI workflow tools.
  • Interests: Global travel, triathlon & rock climbing, piano & violin.
  • Languages: English and Spanish.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity Bootcamp 2025 Q2 is an eight‑week, hands‑on program that equips professionals and researchers with practical skills to deploy AI in everyday projects—without writing code.

Key Skills You’ll Gain

  • Create websites, documents, and presentations with AI assistance.
  • Conduct literature reviews, analyze data, and build dashboards.
  • Design custom chatbots for domain‑specific Q&A.
  • Automate workflows that run around the clock.
  • Generate and edit images, videos, and design materials.
  • Develop AI solutions that integrate seamlessly into daily work.
