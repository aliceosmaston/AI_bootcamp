About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity Bootcamp 2025 Q2 is an eight‑week, hands‑on program that equips professionals and researchers with practical skills to deploy AI in everyday projects—without writing code.

Key Skills You’ll Gain

  • Create websites, documents, and presentations with AI assistance.
  • Conduct literature reviews, analyze data, and build dashboards.
  • Design custom chatbots for domain‑specific Q&A.
  • Automate workflows that run around the clock.
  • Generate and edit images, videos, and design materials.
  • Develop AI solutions that integrate seamlessly into daily work.
